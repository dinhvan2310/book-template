Dế mèn phiêu lưu ký – Tô Hoài

The classic work Cricket's Adventure by writer To Hoai is closely associated with the childhood of many people.

The Adventures of Cricket are considered exemplary pages of children's literature. It seems that every sentence, paragraph, and image has a strong impact on the reader's aesthetic thoughts and feelings. The work describes the adventures of a Cricket through the animal and human worlds.

Hot issues such as: good and evil, war and peace, ideals and reason for life are expressed gently, delicately and deeply. Men has gone through adventures in the animal world, overcoming countless risks and events, but step by step Men has adjusted and self-aware to become a person rich in ideals and desires. understanding and the resilient bravery of a young man with his head held high and his feet on the ground.

We will get lost in the close and dear animal world with all the animals closely associated with rustic rural life such as Uncle Xien Toc, Mantis Fighter, Elephant Grasshopper, Green Frog, Dragonfly... To Hoai's talent is demonstrated in his ability to capture and describe the psychological life of children and young adults. He "painted" a world with countless new emotions, practical and adventurous desires, and delicate vibrations before the beauty of life and nature.

Author

Tô Hoài

To Hoai is a famous Vietnamese writer. He was born in 1920 and died in 2014. To Hoai is known for his work "The Adventures of Cricket" -one of his most famous works. This work has been associated with many people's childhood and is considered one of the exemplary children's literature. To Hoai succeeded in depicting the adventures of a Cricket through the animal and human world. The work also presents issues such as good and bad, war and peace, ideals and reasons for living. To Hoai used his talent to create a world full of emotions and wonder at the beauty of life and nature.
