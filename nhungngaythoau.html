Những ngày thơ ấu – Nguyên Hồng

Childhood Days is a touching work by author Nguyen Hong. This book brings readers unique experiences of childhood, the "extreme vibrations of a young soul". With stories full of tears and attached to each person's childhood, Childhood Days has become an excellent work loved by many readers. From the sound of the trumpet to the mother's womb, to Christmas Eve and one small step, every page of the book is emotional and full of meaning. If you are looking for an emotional and meaningful book to read, Childhood Days is the perfect choice for you.

Childhood Days is a patchwork story about the author's own painful childhood. Hong - a boy born into a rich but not very happy family. From an early age, he understood the indifference and irresponsibility of his drug-addicted father, and the pain and humiliation of his mother when she was always trampled on and tortured by the whole family to the point of having to abandon her family. All the children went abroad to seek food. With simple and clear language, Childhood Days not only brings readers a simple story, close to everyday life, but it also portrays the feudal and antiquated social context of Vietnam during the period. old.

Author

Nguyên Hồng

Writer Nguyen Hong's real name is Nguyen Nguyen Hong, he was born on November 5, 1918 in a Catholic family in Nam Dinh city. His father died early, his family was poor, and Nguyen Hong had to drop out of school. In 1935, he and his mother went to Hai Phong, and once again lived in working-class hamlets such as Cam hamlet and Dong Khe Pagoda hamlet. It was that difficult childhood that he conveyed intact in his memoir. Childhood Days is not about a peaceful, happy childhood, but rather bitter stories through the perspective of Hong - an innocent, immature child who suffered a lot of estrangement from his relatives. family.
