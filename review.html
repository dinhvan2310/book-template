A classic literary work, demonstrating the talent and sophistication of writer To Hoai in creating attractive characters and stories.

"The Adventures of Cricket" is a work that established the name of writer To Hoai in Vietnamese literature before 1945. In 2021, writer To Hoai's Cricket turned eighty years old. “Uncle” is an immortal literary character. Although "The Adventures of Cricket" belongs to the genre of "fairy tales", although Cricket is an animal, with the method of "humanization" it was built by writer To Hoai as a literary character equal to the human world. People.

"The Adventures of Cricket" by writer To Hoai conquered many generations of readers first of all because of its deeply humanistic content. Behind the work are lessons that are still valid today: Every free individual must be a member of a free society; The spirit of humanity, tolerance, equality, charity, and solidarity creates lasting strength for each individual and community; Conflict and war must be eliminated; We must build a society in the spirit of peace and harmony; Must build comprehensive personalities...

The formation of each citizen's personality is a harmonious combination of the educational roles of family, school and society. Literature has "soft power" in building behavioral culture, but not everyone is fully aware of this important issue. Let's work together to nurture love, as a noble cultural behavior, right in school with the beauty of eternal literature.
